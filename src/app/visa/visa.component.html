Process of VISA applying:

  • 1. Date of Travel: You must be clear about your travelling dates. One must have journey tickets available before applying for a visa. .
  • 2. Time Frame: Although processing of VISA doesn’t take longer time but you still need to apply VISA as early as possible, generally 30 Days in advance. As an Embassy can take some longer time to issue VISA for travelling country.
  • 3. Documentation: Before applying VISA, You should have all documents ready while you apply for a visa. Your passport is the most important document you will need, it should have 06 months validity post your travel dates. Kindly check list of travel documents required to apply VISA from Embassy website of related country.
  • 4. Visa Fees: Once you documents are complete, and apply VISA, you have to pay VISA fee, which is different for individual country.
  • 5. Track your visa: After VISA apply, one can track his/her VISA application online. You just need to fill your reference number, and you will know the real-time status of your application.
  • 6. Foreign Exchange : After all the formalities for visa process, another important task is Foreign Exchange. It is always recommended to carry out the local currency of the country /place you are going to visit. One can also buy a Forex Card, as it is the most convenient way of carrying foreign currency. There are many Card service providers which can arrange Travelling Card for your visit.
