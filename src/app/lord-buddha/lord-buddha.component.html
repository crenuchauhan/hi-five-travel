GOA

One of Indias most popular seaside destinations, Goa offers a heady mix of warm azure waters, white sand beaches, atmospheric cafs, electronic festivals, vibrant markets and antiquated colonial architecture a legacy of its time under Portuguese rule. Highlights include the elegant historical churches of Old Goa and the spice plantations near Ponda, as well as several wildlife sanctuaries, which make up approximately one fifth of the entire, state, and encompass a wealth of bird and animal life.
