Vibrant Rajasthan with Golden Triangle

(08 Nights/09 Days)

Destinations: Delhi –Udaipur –Ranakpur –Udaipur –Jodhpur –Jaipur -Agra – Delhi

Day 01 : Arrive Delhi

Arrive Delhi Internationalairport and meet our tour representative with Indian Traditional Style. Then transfer you to hotel.

Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 02 : Delhi

In the morning after breakfast visit Red fort, Jama Masjid and Raj Ghat- the place where Gandhi ji got cremation, Chandni Chowk and spice market. After this we will take lunch at local restaurant and then visit India Gate, Qutub Minar and Lotus Temple. In the Evening free for leisure.

Overnight stay at Hotel.

Day 03 : Delhi – Udaipur by flight

In the morning after breakfast visit Humayun Tomb – this is a famous UNESCO world heritage site and protected by ASI. You can see there the beautiful Islamic architecture and significance of that place after that we will take lunch at local restaurant and transfer to airport for taking the flight for Udaipur.

Arrive at the Udaipur airport, and then transfer to hotel. Take some rest, in the evening, enjoy boat ride at Lake Pichola. Come back and overnight stay at hotel.

Day 04 : Udaipur

After a breakfast at hotel, then proceed to city tour visit city palace, Jagdish Temple and Feteh Sagar Lake, then come back to hotel for lunch & take some rest, post lunch go for camel ride through the village and Aravelli hills. In the evening free for explore the local markets.

Overnight Stay at hotel.

Day 05 : Udaipur – Ranakpur – Jodhpur

In the morning after breakfast continue our tour towards, Jodhpur via Ranakpur. Visit Jain temple, superbly preserved in a near-perfect condition for over 500 years. The most attractive features are the 29 halls of the temple, which have 1444 pillars of which no two are like. Then proceed towards Jodhpur. After reach Jodhpur visit Jaswant thada, Mandor Garden and in the evening visit Sardar Girdkot market.

Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 06 : Jodhpur

In the morning after breakfast visit Mehrangarh fort-It was founded by Rao Jodha in 1459.it is one of the largest fort in India. After this visit government museum and Umaid Bhawan palace and museum. In the evening free for exploring the beautiful markets.

Overnight stay at Jodhpur.

Day 07 : Jodhpur – Jaipur

Early morning drive to Jaipur by road its 6 hours long drive. Jaipur is known as ‘Pink City’. After reach in that vibrant city visit Amber Fort by jeep and Hawa Mahal from outside. Overnight at hotel.

Day 08 : Jaipur– Agra

In the morning after taking breakfast visit city palace and Jantar Mantar (Observatory) – this is also a UNESCO heritage site and built by the Rajput king Sawai Jai Singh. It was completed in 1738 CE. Afternoon drive to Agra. Overnight at Agra.

Day 09 : Agra – Delhi

Agra, a city in northern India’s Uttar Pradesh state, is home to the iconic Taj Mahal, a mausoleum built for the Mughal ruler Shah Jahan’s wife, Mumtaz Mahal, who died in childbirth in 1631. In the morning after breakfast visit Morning after taking breakfast visit Taj Mahal (A famous UNESCO world heritage site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World), after visit that magnificent building visit Agra Fort (which is also a UNESCO heritage site) - Where you can see beautiful and stunning Mughal arctechture. Post lunch drive towards Delhi. It’s a 4 hour drive. In the evening after dinner transfer to Delhi airport.
